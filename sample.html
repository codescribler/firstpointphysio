The Thinking

Built on the StoryBrand Framework

StoryBrand is a messaging framework used by thousands of businesses worldwide. The core idea: your customer is the hero of the story — not your company. Your job is to be the guide who helps them win. When a website follows this structure, visitors immediately understand what you offer, how it helps them, and what to do next.

1

Name the Problem

The homepage opens by acknowledging the Practice Manager's real pain — GP overload, recruitment nightmares, wasted ARRS funding. This creates instant recognition: "they understand me."

2

Position the Guide

First Point Physio is presented as the experienced partner with empathy and authority — Band 7+ FCPs, 3 regions, £30 vs £90. Not bragging, but proving you can solve their problem.

3

Give Them a Plan

A clear 3-step process — "Book a Discovery Call → We Handle Everything → Your GPs Get Their Time Back" — plus one repeating CTA throughout. Simplicity removes friction.

The Demo

10 Conversion-Focused Sections, All Working Together

Every section of the homepage has a strategic job. Here's what each one does and why it's there.

01

Hero Header

Passes the "grunt test" — visitors know what you do, how it helps them, and what to do next within 5 seconds of landing.

02

Trust Marquee

Scrolling social proof bar — Band 7+ FCPs, 3 regions, ARRS aligned. Builds credibility immediately after the hero.

03

Stakes Section

Names the pain so visitors feel understood — GP burnout, ARRS waste, recruitment struggles. Empathy before the pitch.

04

Value Proposition

Four benefits in clear cards — GP time reclaimed, funding utilised, zero burden, better outcomes. Answers "what's in it for me?"

05

Guide Section

Empathy statement + testimonial + stats bar. Establishes First Point Physio as the trusted expert with proof, not claims.

06

3-Step Plan

Removes complexity — "Book, We Handle It, GPs Get Time Back." When the path is clear, people take the next step.

07

Explainer Paragraph

The full brand one-liner in paragraph form. For visitors who want to read the complete picture in one go before deciding.

08

Case Study

Real results with metrics — 30% reduction, 48 appointments freed, 92% satisfaction. Social proof with numbers converts.

09

Service & Pricing

Everything-included checklist plus the £30 vs £90 comparison card. Removes ambiguity about what they get.

10

Lead Generator

Free PDF download with email capture. For visitors who aren't ready to call — gives them a reason to exchange their email.
