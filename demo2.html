Sound Familiar?

Your GPs are spending up to 30% of their appointments on musculoskeletal complaints they weren't trained to manage in depth. Meanwhile, waiting lists grow, patients bounce between referrals, and the pressure never lets up.

You've tried the large providers before. The clinician changes every few months. Nobody knows the practice. The phone just rings.

Recruitment on your own is a nightmare. Finding, hiring, and supervising a qualified First Contact Practitioner takes months you don't have, and when they leave, you start again from scratch.

You don't need another faceless provider. You need a partner who knows your practice by name.

What Changes When You Work With Us

GP Time Reclaimed

MSK patients are seen by specialist physiotherapy clinicians embedded in your surgery. Your GPs get that 30% of their day back to focus on complex care.

ARRS Funding Put to Work

Our service is fully funded within the Additional Roles Reimbursement Scheme. No hidden costs, no invoicing surprises. Your ARRS allocation works the way it should.

Zero Operational Burden

We handle every operational detail: recruitment, clinical supervision, appraisals, annual leave cover, and ongoing professional development. You just provide the room.

A Partner, Not a Provider

We're a small, focused team. Our director answers his phone. Your clinician stays. We learn how your practice works and become part of it. That's the difference.

We Know What It's Like
