What Changes When You Work With Us

A fully managed service that gives your GPs time back and puts ARRS funding to work — with none of the operational burden.

GP Time Reclaimed

Our FCPs see MSK patients directly — no GP triage needed. That means fewer appointments clogging your books and more capacity for complex cases that genuinely need a doctor.

ARRS Funding Put to Work

We operate entirely within your ARRS allocation. No surprise costs, no top-ups. You get a senior clinician embedded in your team, fully funded by the budget that's already there.

Zero Operational Burden

Recruitment, onboarding, supervision, CPD, clinical governance — we handle all of it. Your practice manager gets time back, and you get a clinician who's ready from day one.

A Partner, Not a Provider

We don't rotate staff. Our clinicians embed in your team, learn your patient population, and stay. That continuity builds trust — with your patients and your GPs.
